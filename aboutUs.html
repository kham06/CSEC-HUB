‘CSEC HUB’ was created by a group of intelligent Vincentian Students who either have already taken or are preparing to take their CSEC Examinations. The resources provided at school aren't always helpful enough for kids to be able to study for exams. Due to our collective personal experience, we all understand how stressful and unforeseen Exams can be, which is why we joined together to build this website. Our number one Goal is to make preparing for an important and life changing Exam such as CSEC to become less stressful and easier for Caribbean Students. Students already deal with so many other School-Related work, such as SBAs, School Projects, Homework, of course actually studying for the Exams and so much more. Making time for yourself as a student is crucial, especially in times like these. Even students have a personal life and stressing about not knowing where you can get Notes from to prepare for Exams can cause a series of different mental conditions and physical problems. Some of the most common mental issues associated with School are Anxiety Disorders, Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD) and Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and the most common physical issues are stress, headaches, exhaustion, sleep deprivation, weight loss and stomach problems. We want to prevent our Students from going through all these unnecessary problems. With ‘CSEC HUB’, accessing helpful materials will be at the tip of our users fingertips. Visit ‘CSEC HUB’ today to join us in our journey towards a happier, healthier and more productive School Life.
